Privacy Policy for Healthify

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Healthify collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection
- We collect only the data you provide (e.g., health logs, profile info) and anonymous analytics.
- We do not collect sensitive personal data without your consent.

2. Data Usage
- Data is used to provide app features, personalize your experience, and improve the app.

3. Ads
- Healthify uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device and usage data for ad targeting. See Google’s privacy policy for details.

4. Security
- Your data is stored securely using Firebase and protected by authentication.

5. Your Choices
- You can delete your account and data at any time from the app settings.

6. Contact
- For privacy questions, contact: healthify.support@email.com

By using Healthify, you agree to this policy. We may update it as needed. Latest version is always available in the app.
